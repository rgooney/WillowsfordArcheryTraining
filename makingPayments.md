# Willowsford Making Payments Doc
## Making a Payment

1. Members will need to first sign into their account and then navigate to their member dashboard.
2. Once inside member dashboard, under the **Payment Portal**, members will be able to enter in the amount they owe to the club.
3. They will need to fill the **Membership Dues** and **Payment Amount** fields in $USD, then select either **PayPal**, **Venmo**, **Pay Later** or **Debit or Credit Card** for the payment option
4. Members will then be taken to the affiliated **Payment Portal** with their selected payment option, to which they will enter their credentials to initialize the transaction for their membership dues. 
5. Once the payment is successfully processed, an email will be sent to the **Treasurer**, as well as a receipt of the payment, to the member.
